# Bibliography

Entries here identify external sources. Inclusion is not independent
verification, reproduction, or endorsement of a computational proof.

## E1997

*Discrete Mathematics* **165–166** (1997), 227–231.

- Classification in this project: source for an external open conjecture.
- Project verification: bibliographic identification only.

## HSS2025

A. S. Hegde, R. B. Sandeep, and P. Shashank, “Erdős–Gyárfás conjecture on
graphs without long induced paths,” arXiv:2410.22842v2 (2025).
[arXiv:2410.22842v2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.22842v2).

- Classification in this project: source for
  `EXTERNAL_COMPUTATIONAL_RESULT` claims concerning P13-free graphs and the
  P12-free C4/C8 statement.
- Project verification: not reproduced or independently certified.
